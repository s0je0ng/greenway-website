Sustainable & Low-waste

Accommodations

For a sustainable and healthy environment, stay at sustainable and low-waste accommodations when traveling in Amsterdam.

01

Conscious Hotels

Conscious Hotels are designed with sustainability in mind, from the use of eco-friendly materials to energy-efficient systems. The hotels operate on 100% renewable energy and focus on waste reduction through recycling programs and offering organic, locally sourced food options in their restaurants.

02

Hotel Jakarta Amsterdam

Hotel Jakarta Amsterdam is a sustainable hotel built with environmentally friendly materials and boasts a tropical garden maintained through a closed-loop water system. The hotel uses solar panels for energy and promotes waste reduction through recycling and minimal use of single-use plastics.

View Website

03

Ecomama Hotel

Ecomama Hotel is committed to sustainability, using recycled materials in construction and eco-friendly products throughout. The hotel features a green roof and a sustainable water system, providing a unique and environmentally conscious stay for its guests.

View Website

04

Cocomama

Cocomama is a boutique hostel that emphasizes sustainability through eco-friendly design and practices. They use recycled materials, offer energy-efficient amenities, and have a garden where guests can compost their food waste. The hostel also supports local and fair-trade products.

View Website

05

Volkshotel

Volkshotel incorporates sustainability into its operations by using renewable energy, recycling, and offering locally sourced food in their restaurant. The hotel promotes eco-friendly transportation options and supports community initiatives focused on sustainability.

View Website

06

Element Amsterdam

Element Amsterdam is part of a hotel chain known for its commitment to sustainability. The hotel features energy-efficient systems, uses sustainable materials in construction and furnishings, and offers healthy, locally sourced food options. They also focus on waste reduction through comprehensive recycling programs.

View Website

07

The Dylan Amsterdam

The Dylan Amsterdam is a luxury boutique hotel that emphasizes sustainability through eco-friendly practices. The hotel uses energy-efficient lighting and heating, sources food locally, and has a waste reduction program that includes recycling and composting.

08

Zoku Amsterdam

Lavinia Good Food focuses on healthy, organic, and locally sourced ingredients. Their menu includes many plant-based options, and they emphasize sustainability by minimizing food waste and using eco-friendly packaging.
